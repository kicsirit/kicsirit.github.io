Moments on the Camino:

Backpack

Most important things you will need:
  • comfortable shoes
  • sandals so that you could change once in a while
  • 2 breathable T-shirts
  • 1 pair of light trousers for hiking
  • 1 pair of shorts
  • 3 pairs of seamless socks
  • 3 underwears
  • 1 light towel
  • small shover gel and shampoo
  • for possibles blisters: strips, betadine, needle, thread

The ideal weight of the backpack is 10% of our weight. Be careful with your backpack, it should be too heavy. The lesser it weighs, the easier will be for you.

Boots

It doesn´t have to be the tipical boots for hiking of Decathlon. Use the shoes that are more confortable for your feet.

It is important not to use a brand new shoes without trying walking in them. Before the Camino you should train your new shoues and go for a walk sometimes.

Money

You have to calculate with the following expense:
  • plane tickets or/and train tickets, bus tickets
  • accomodation price, see it later
  • meals: breakfast aprox. 5 euro, pilgrim menu 10-14 euro

Accomodation

According to your choice, there are 3 types of accomodation that you can find in almost every town:
  • Albergue Municipal 5-8 euros
  • Albergue Privado 8-15 euros
  • Hostal/Hotel 30-60 euros

In the albergues mostly there are bunk beds, so if you want you pick down you better hurry, because the beds you get "first arrive first sirve". In the albergues you can not reserve in advance.

Tipps

If you have a blister that bothers you, poke it with the needle with a thread in it and leave it there during the night so that the liquid leave without tear your skin.

Don´t try to go faster than it is comfortable for you just because you want to keep up with someone. Everybody has it´s own rate. You will see each other later in the next town or in the end of the stage.

It is recommended to start in the morning the earliest possible, because - in summertime - it can get hot early. And also because people normally starts early, they get early to the destination of the day and the albergues get full quickly.
